\nthat leads us astray.<\/p>\n

\nand fall madly off track in the wrong directions.<\/p>\n

\nand live in the sepia toned world.<\/p>\n

\nwithout this beating fragrant thing.<\/p>\n

\nwe merely dry up.<\/p>\n

\nand weak.<\/p>\n
