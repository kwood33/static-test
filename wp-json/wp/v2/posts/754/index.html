Being deliberate about what you consume means\u00a0shutting out a lot of incoming elements.<\/p>\n

Curating your Facebook feed isn’t enough to become mindful about what you’re letting into your life. Your TV is evil. Drama is an addiction.<\/p>\n

Only the truth will set you free.<\/p>\n

The secret to cultivating this truth lies in curation and creation. Living from a place of true freedom of thought. However, this can only come when we begin to shape our own filters.<\/p>\n

You\u00a0decide what to\u00a0let into your\u00a0mind. Take control of the stream that’s coming into your\u00a0life. Fill your life with things that uplift you. Everything else is meaningless noise.<\/p>\n

We live in a world that prizes efficiency at all costs. But, what expense are we incurring?<\/p>\n

By plugging ourselves into the incessant flow of information we become products of this machine.<\/p>\n

Only by conscious removal can we get our freedom back.<\/p>\n

Choose the best books you want to read. Unplug from any flow of information you didn’t take the time to create yourself. It’s difficult.<\/p>\n

You’ll face massive resistance, but your freedom is at stake. Isn’t that worth it?<\/p>\n

Disconnect, unplug and rejoice.<\/p>\n

It won’t be easy, the pull of our addictions\u00a0is too much to handle sometimes.<\/p>\n

Just know this. You will make it out alive, and this time you may even have a little more of your humanity intact.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
