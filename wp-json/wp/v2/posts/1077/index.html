Writing is the truest form of magic.<\/p>\n

it creates battles more real than the ones we fight internally every single day, <\/em><\/p>\n

it gives us worlds more real than our own, <\/em><\/p>\n

it turns us inward and makes it look outward, <\/em><\/p>\n

it lets us\u00a0talk to ghosts, <\/em><\/p>\n

we\u00a0commune with the dead when we\u00a0find words better off unsaid,<\/em><\/p>\n

we\u00a0find the deepest reaches and look into the dark corners of our\u00a0soul, <\/em><\/p>\n

it finds us\u00a0when we\u00a0can\u2019t find ourselves, <\/em><\/p>\n

it creates a future for us to step into, <\/em><\/p>\n

it looks into the past to see what we\u2019ve done, <\/em><\/p>\n

it furls and folds and hurls into our emotions, <\/em><\/p>\n

tears aren\u2019t the only emotion found, <\/em><\/p>\n

it tells lies, and tells the truth, <\/em><\/p>\n

it finds our\u00a0heart before it starts beating, <\/em><\/p>\n

it cries tears before we\u00a0start breathing, <\/em><\/p>\n

it knows us\u00a0before we\u00a0know ourselves, <\/em><\/p>\n

it creates books to put on the shelf, to look at collections of brain matter, <\/em><\/p>\n

every single fiber and stitch and moment gets woven into an eternal thread, only lost to fire, <\/em><\/p>\n

in bits and bytes, paper and pen, the words are meaningful even when we start again, <\/em><\/p>\n

even when we can\u2019t find our way they help to direct us back home,<\/em><\/p>\n

it creates bonds and friendships, with the real and imaginary, <\/em><\/p>\n

it locates us\u00a0in a world where we\u00a0feel lost, it teaches us\u00a0to teach ourselves, it opens and closes, it heals and makes us\u00a0feel.<\/em><\/p>\n

Words are my life and I hope they\u2019re yours too.\u00a0A record of our own lives, seen through our thoughts and through our minds, made true with our hearts and finally the fingertips do the work, the hardest work of all, the work of giving birth.<\/p>\n

My legacy will be written.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
