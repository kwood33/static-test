When we choose our own destiny, we choose the path meant for us all along.<\/p>\n

Instead of externalities shaping our vision we let our inner pull guide us towards the direction that is best. This usness<\/i> is what the world needs, and is what we need to feel alive and full within our human experience.<\/p>\n

Anything less is simply living the life of another. Shaped by a loop of karmic cause and effect. A purely reactionary lifestyle, never creating or doing anything of true value.<\/p>\n

It takes\u00a0courage to step out against the masses of sheep. Not to become a wolf and prey upon the masses. It’s not a zone of judgement. It’s one of liberation. You become the lighthouse that cuts through the fog.<\/p>\n

You prevent others from living the same mindless life again and again, all through being you.<\/i> Refusing to compromise on what you hold most dear.<\/p>\n

When you do this you allow others to tap into the fullest within themselves. When you take off your own veil and step out of the collective illusion, you provide deep support for others willing (or too afraid) to do the same.<\/p>\n

Be brilliant. Let the power of choice direct your life. The freedom that comes from taking responsibility for your own life comes packed with uncertainty and doubt, but the taste of greater possibility outweighs the slight queasiness.<\/p>\n

Choose to step into your power. Do you to the fullest extent. Embrace your quirkiness and strange passions, the world needs it. We need you to come alive and help set the world on fire.<\/p>\n

Ignite the spark within your core to stay committed to this path of passionate, free living. The current that runs beneath your life will allow more beauty to come to the forefront. It’s as if a godlike wind is directing your sails.<\/p>\n

Blaze your path. Others will follow, hopefully on a trail of their own.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
