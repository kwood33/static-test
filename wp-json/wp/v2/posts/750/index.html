Freedom is all about choice.<\/p>\n

As a society\u00a0we’re cultured into fear. I used to be afraid to leave the classroom to go to the bathroom. I was also painfully shy (so your feelings of fear might be a little different).<\/p>\n

As I got older, I realized most systems I was living in were filled with a bunch of bullshitters. Instead of doing anything real the focus was on blending, on talking out of your ass. On doing just enough\u00a0to go unnoticed.<\/p>\n

On maintaining a status quo. Personal freedom is bad for business.<\/p>\n

Instead of choosing, we’re taught to pick pre-made tracks.<\/p>\n

I remember the feeling of making an actual choice for myself. It was after my dad died. I was 20 years old.<\/p>\n

For 20 years I managed to pass off my decision making. Nice work.<\/p>\n

I didn’t know what to do. I was anxious. I thought I was going to die. I was definitely going to fuck up.<\/p>\n

I did it anyways.<\/p>\n

I taught myself how to program, I dove deep into philosophy and spirituality, and I started to meditate. Through intentional choice I began to lay the foundation for the world I’m currently rocking.<\/p>\n

If you’re like me then you’re already on the outside. You see through the illusion of every large system imaginable. You see through the illusions in yourself.<\/p>\n

You know it’s time for your badass self to shine.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
